Organizational Archetypes
DORA Delivery Variables
DF — Deployment Frequency 4.0
Deployments per day. Elite: >1/day. Low: <1/month.
LT — Lead Time for Changes (hrs) 4.0
Hours from commit to production. Elite: <1hr. Low: >1 month.
CFR — Change Failure Rate 0.05
Fraction of changes causing incidents. Elite: 0–5%. Low: 46–60%.
MTTR — Mean Time to Restore (hrs) 0.5
Hours to recover from failure. Elite: <1hr. Low: >1 week.
Organizational Perturbation Terms
M — Developer Morale Multiplier 1.0
Ratio of energizing work to chronic toil. <1 = degradation regime.
U — Management Urgency 1.0
Externally imposed schedule pressure. Applied as exponent on (1−CFR)^U². Proceed with caution.
Cm — Competence Mismatch 0.1
Misalignment of responsibility, authority, and actual capability.
E — Executive Volatility 0.1
Strategic oscillation, reprioritization churn, initiative reversals.
Debt and Remediation
TDR — Technical Debt Ratio 0.1
Fraction of capacity consumed by historical shortcuts and rework burden.
R — Remediation Rate 0.1
Capacity diverted to debt repayment. Subtracts from visible throughput; benefits are deferred.
Value and Sensitivity Constants
V — Business Value per Change 1.0
Normalized realized value delivered per change. Relative unit.
λ — Debt sensitivity 1.0
How aggressively TDR attenuates output via e^(−λ·TDR).
α — Competence sensitivity 1.0
Cm's weight in the Ω coherence term.
β — Volatility sensitivity 1.0
E's weight in the Ω coherence term.
